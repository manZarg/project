LOVELY & BEAUTIFUL WATERFALLS


Bohol is a spectacular island with a varied landscape and a plethora of attraction to see. So, here are some of Boholano's wonderful water falls that gives you a feeling of freshness and peace. It's time to explore and discover the paradise of "Boholano's Water Falls" :

Ingkumhan Falls | Dimiao, Bohol

Located: Dimiao, Bohol, Philippines

Ingkumhan Falls is one of Bohol's most beautiful waterfalls and a must-see for visitors. The ride up to Ingkumhan falls is quite exciting and well worth the long drive. Because the/assets water is so clear, you might even see some fish around. There are cliff leaps and rope swings, an area where is the best to jump off and are only two of the many reasons why this Bohol tourist attraction site is one of the top locations to visit!

Kinahugan Falls | Jagna, Bohol


Watch Full Video

Located: Sitio Karap-agan, Brgy. Cabungaan, Jagna, Bohol, Philippines

In a little hilly sitio of Karap-agan, the magnificent Kinahugan waterfalls can be found. From the Poblacion, the trail winds up for about five kilometers. It takes only one and a half hours to walk there on foot. It's impossible not to be impressed by how these magnificent waterfalls were produced. In nature, the three waterfalls are trigonomial. The center fall is flanked by two other falls, one on the cliff's southern dice, all of which are almost completely covered in moss. The cliff reaches a height of approximately 50 feet. The three waterfalls combine to form a large basin that is about 15 feet deep and 60 feet in diameter. This gorgeous spot's one-of-a-kind appeal sets it apart from the rest of Bohol's waterfalls. The cascading rivers, moss-covered rocks, and lush greenery that surrounds you will help you relax. With the breathtaking blue sky above and the crystal clear waters below, you will undoubtedly feel as if you are in heaven.

Can-Umantad Falls | Candijay, Bohol


Watch Full Video

Located: Candijay, Bohol, Philippines

The largest cascades reaches a height of 60 feet and is divided into three layers. The water flows easily into a rocky basin that is very shallow. A lower waterfall a few meters downstream is much smaller at 10 feet high, but it drops into a deep green circular lake, a favorite spot for those who enjoy a short swim.

Dimiao Twin Falls (Pahangog Falls)| Dimiao, Bohol

Located: Dimiao, Bohol, Philippines

Dimiao falls are a pair of 25-meter-high waterfalls that plunge down a limestone rock face and into a vast catch basin below. The turquoise natural-made swimming pool is deep enough for some rock jumping in some sections, but take sure to check the depth beforehand. Floating around the rock pools in a rubber ring is one of the best things to do at "Dimiao Twin Falls". You can also get a free head massage by swimming up under the falls. A tiny sandy islet and a cave lie between the two sets of falls, which you can discover.
