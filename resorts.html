Must Visit Resorts in Bohol

Bohol is blessed with beautiful beaches that many tourists loved to visit. Besides from its crystal-blue sea water, you will also feel at home by the hospitable accommodations. Anyone will be mesmerized by the beauty each resort offers.
Here are some of the resorts in Bohol that should be included in your bucket list.

Henann Resort Alona Beach

Located: Alona Beach, Tawala, Panglao Island, Bohol, Philippines, 6340
The Hennan Resort Alona Beach is the biggest Bohol beach resort in Panglao Island. This beach resort offers 400 luxurious accommodations, which include rooms and villas. The rooms have complete amenities such as a bathroom with complete toiletries, cable television, free WiFi, an aircon, and an in-room refrigerator. There is also plenty of restaurants to choose from inside this beach resort, such as the Sea Breeze Café, Coral Café, and Christine’s Western Cuisine.

Furthermore, this beach resort is strategically located in the widely recognized stretch of sand, which is Alona Beach. You can expect to be surrounded by multiple restaurants, cafes, and shops in the middle of a vibrant environment.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

Henann Resort Alona Beach

Amorita Resort

Located: Ester Lim Drive, Barangay Tawala, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6340 Panglao Island, Philippines
Amorita Resort is a great place to surround yourself with beach scenery. What makes this resort unique is it is the only one with a full view of the Panglao Sea’s crystal-blue waters. You have the option to rent a room or villa. The rooms and villas have all the necessary amenities such as a bathroom, cable television, mini-refrigerator, minibar, free WiFi, and aircon. There are also plenty of restaurants you can try in this beach resort.

Moreover, there are two swimming pools you can take a dip in, such as the infinity pool and the dive shop where beginner diving classes are held. However, if you are looking to swim on a beach, Alona Beach is not that far as such can be reached through walking distance.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

Amorita Resort

BE Grand Resort Bohol

Located: Barangay Danao, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6340 Panglao Island, Philippines
The BE Grand Resort Bohol offers a luxurious and relaxing experience. This Bohol resort is located on Panglao Island. You will have the chance to choose between 208 fancy guest rooms and 19 luxurious villas. On the one hand, the guest rooms have relaxing views, which can either be an ocean/pool or garden view. The villas are surrounded by blue lagoons. The rooms and villas have complete amenities such as a bathroom with complete toiletries, cable television, aircon, free WiFi, and a terrace for relaxation purposes.

This Bohol resort offers plenty of entertainment. Some of the swimming activities you can engage in are studying diving in the dive pool, taking a dip in the swimming pool, and canoeing in the sea. You can also relax in the spa, enjoy in the game room, and exercise in the gym. And if you are looking to swim in the sea, a nearby beach you can go to is Gakot White Beach.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

BE Grand Resort Bohol

The Bellevue Resort

Located: Barangay Doljo, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6340, Panglao Island, Phillipines
The Bellevue Resort serves as a serene getaway from city noise. This Bohol beach resort is located in Panglao and is recognized as an ASEAN Green Hotel awardee.
Bellevue’s rooms have varying breathtaking views such as the waterfront and forest. All the beach resort’s rooms are filled with basic amenities such as a bathroom, minibar, cable television, pod dock speaker, free WiFi, and aircon. There are also plenty of amenities for activities inside the hotel such as swimming in the pool, exercising in the gym, playing beach football, and getting dive lessons.

If you have grown tired of these hobbies, this beach resort schedules uniquely fun activities for its guests daily. Some of the activities they have done are kite flying, face painting, Koi fish feeding, practicing calligraphy, and doing sunset yoga. There are also nearby tourist spots in your area such as Balicasag Reef and Hinagdanan Cave.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

The Bellevue Resort

Eskaya Beach Resort and Spa

Located: Barangay Tawala, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6340 Panglao Island, Philippines
Another resort in Panglao Island you can stay in is the Eskaya Beach Resort and Spa. The word “Eskaya” refers to Bohol’s only recognized indigenous people. This Bohol beach resort is known for having villas that are made of strong construction materials and indigenous equipment. All villas have basic amenities such as a bathroom with complete toiletries, swimming pool, minibar, cable television with a DVD player, an aircon, and iPod speaker. This resort also has a restaurant named Lantawan. Eating in Lantawan is an excellent way to further experience Filipino and Boholano culture through the dishes. You can also expect international cuisines to be served from time to time.

While it seems that staying in this beach resort is relaxing enough, another available option is to participate in Bohol tours such as dolphin watching at Pamilacan Island and firefly watching at Loay Town.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

Eskaya Beach Resort and Spa

South Palms Resort Panglao

Located: Barangay Balod, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6340 Panglao Island, Philippines
The South Palms Resort is a testament to Boholano culture and local architecture. This Bohol beach resort is located on Panglao Island, where you have a complete view of the Bohol Sea. You have 90 units to choose from such as rooms and villas. Each one has unique views of the vicinity such as the beachfront, garden, and swimming pool. The rooms and villas have a bathroom with basic toiletries, flat-screen cable television, terrace, mini-refrigerator, and an aircon. And there are also two types of restaurants to choose from such as the Oceanica Seafood Restaurant and Coast Pool Bar.

Go on Bohol island-hopping adventures which will take guests to several snorkeling spots such as Pamilacan Island, Habagat Shipwreck, Cabilao Island, and Balicasag Island.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

South Palms Resort Panglao

Anda White Beach Resort

Located: Sitio Dagohoy, Barangay Bacong, Bohol, 6311 Anda, Philippines
Located directly on Anda Beach, Anda White Beach Resort is an hour and 45 minutes’ drive from Tagbilaran City. It features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Air-conditioned rooms have private balconies and DVD players. Fitted with sliding glass doors and wooden furnishings, they come with a minibar, safe and an attached bathroom.

Guests may snorkel and fish in clear waters bordering the resort. Other leisure options include relaxing poolside massages and games of billiards and darts. Evening drinks can be enjoyed at Fizz Buzz bar after hearty local and international meals in the restaurant. Meals can also be served in rooms.
For more information, you can visit their official facebook page:

Anda White Beach Resort

Casa Amihan

Located: Sition Lourdes, Barangay Virgen, Bohol, 6311 Anda, Philippines
Only steps away from the beautiful turquoise waters, Casa Amihan provides guests with free WiFi, and elegantly furnished rooms. Guests can enjoy a quiet afternoon relaxing by the private beach area or go for a swim in the outdoor pool. Rooms are well-appointed and fitted with en suite bathrooms. Amenities include an outdoor seating area, air conditioning and coffee/tea making facilities. A shower and hairdryer are included in all the bathrooms.

Staff at Casa Amihan can arrange for various activities including snorkeling, kayaking and diving. This beachfront property also provides guests with airport transfers, tours and excursions. And also there are an array of local and international cuisines can be enjoyed at the on-site restaurant accompanied by beautiful sea views.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

Casa Amihan

Mithi Resort and Spa

Located: Barangay Bingag, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6339 Panglao, Philippines
Mithi Resort and Spa is located along the clear coastal waters of Panglao. All the rooms have cable television, a bathroom with complete toiletries, a balcony, and an aircon. Other services include daily housekeeping, in-room dining service, and a free intercontinental breakfast buffet. The resort also offers Antonio’s Bar and Café Luisa for when you plan to have some drinks.

Your luxurious experience in this beach hotel is complemented by plenty of avenues for swimming. You can swim in the sea along with the white sand beach and infinity swimming pool. And this Bohol beach hotel also happens to be nearby the locally famous Hinagdanan Cave.
For more information, you can visit their official website:

Mithi Resort and Spa

Donatela Resort and Sanctuary

Located: Barangay Bingag, Panglao Island, Bohol, 6339 Panglao, Philippines
The Donatela Resort and Sanctuary is located near Alona Beach and Danao Beach. Staying in this Bohol resort is a way to spend more intimate vacations with your loved ones. This is because the rooms that you will be given are in the form of villas. All the villas provide a bathroom with complete toiletries, aircon, cable television, terrace, minibar, Bluetooth music system, dressing room, and free WiFi. The resort also offers a restaurant named Paprika that provides several international cuisines and a bar known as Tropika.

Moreover, what makes people come to this resort is to have a relaxing experience in The Spa Village, which offers its locally famous signature massages such as the Traditional Hilot, Donatela Signature Massage, and Aromatherapy Massage. There are also body scrub packages such as the Red Rice Body Scrub and Bohol Chocolate Body Scrub.
For more information, you can visit their official website:
